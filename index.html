Junior National Law Conference 2019

The Junior National Law Conference, which is the most sought-after annual event of the calendar of the Bar Association of Sri Lanka, was successfully held for the 19th consecutive year under the theme 'e-JNLC 1.0 - Towards a Digital Era in the Legal Profession’ on the 29th, 30th of November 2019 & 1st December 2019 at the ‘The Blue Water Hotel’, Wadduwa.
