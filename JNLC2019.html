Junior National Law Conference 2019

The Junior National Law Conference, which is the most sought-after annual event of the calendar of the Bar Association of Sri Lanka, will be held for the 19th consecutive year under the theme ‘eJNLC 1.0 - Towards a digital era’.

Message from the Chairman of JNLC 2019 - Mr. Kaneel Maddumage AAL

Save the Date

The Junior National Law Conference 2019 will be held on the 29th, 30th of November 2019 & 1st December 2019 at the ‘The Blue Water Hotel ’, Wadduwa. Keep the dates free. 

Message from the Convenor of the Junior Bar Committee, Mr. Nishan Premathiratne
