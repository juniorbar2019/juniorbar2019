JUNIOR NATIONAL LAW CONFERENCE '19

THEME

Following the success of the Junior National Law Conference 2018 where the focus was on equipping the Junior Lawyers with the required knowledge, skills and the right attitude which centered on the theme that Junior Lawyers are the future of the legal profession, this year the Junior National Law Conference would aim at focusing on creating a dialogue towards a digital era in the legal profession. The Conference will be themed “e-JNLC 1.0”, “Towards a digital era in the legal profession”. JNLC 2019 aims to keep in line with the advancing technology and the developing combination of Information Communication Technology with the legal field in many spheres.

The Conference is titled as “e-JNLC 1.0” which indicates the merge between technology and the legal field which will be brought into the Conference this time while computer programming language of binary codes have been used to derive the term ‘1.0’ in the conference title which indicates the first version of e-JNLC.

JNLC 2019 seeks to emphasize the importance of using technology, the need for the legal professionals to keep abreast with the advancing technology and adapting to the developing usage of technology in the legal profession.
